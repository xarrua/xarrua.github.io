Presentation

I am an enthusiastic physiotherapist specialized in the rehabilitation of musculoskeletal and respiratory injuries. My clinical experience includes treating patients with a variety of conditions, from sports injuries to chronic diseases, with experience in preclinical, emergency, and intensive care. I am always looking for ways to improve my patients' outcomes, which has led me to learn more about technology and development.

Nowadays, I combine my experience in physiotherapy with dance skills (Tango) and development to create technological solutions that help patients recover faster and with better results. I have worked on projects to develop mobile and desktop applications to support patients in tracking their treatments, logging their exercises, and receiving guidance on their progress and health in general.

Additionally, I love teaching and sharing my knowledge. I have taught Tango and therapy courses in various countries and hope to integrate progress in technology into these areas. I believe that continuous education is the key to staying up-to-date in such a fast-paced industry as health and technology.

If you are looking for someone who can combine skills in physiotherapy, development, and other alternatives to create innovative solutions in the health field, do not hesitate to contact me. I would love to talk with you about how we can work together to improve people's lives.
